TRUMP SNAKE

Instructions:

The goal of the game is to get as high a score as possible!

Controls: UP (W or ↑) DOWN (S or ↓) LEFT (A or ←) RIGHT (D or →).

Score points by collecting money. The game will be over if The Donald runs in to his own wall or goes outside the bounds of the screen.

To restart the game at any time, press R. Press M to mute/unmute.
